Services

Just as the Prosecution relies on Law Enforcement’s investigations to support their cases, the Defense relies on expert Investigative Services for case support. Through integrity, experience, and excellent communication we ensure every piece of information obtained is delivered to the attorney, assisting in developing and organizing the strongest defense possible for their clients.

Our firm hyper focuses on and specializes in Criminal Defense Investigations. We work with many defense attorneys throughout the state, acquiring small contracts called NRE’s through the Office of Public Defense Services (OPDS), as well as privately retained case work.. Our Firm investigates several fields of law, to include civil, criminal, juvenile, family, fraud, and more.

A Process Server delivers (or serves) legal documents to a defendant or an individual involved in legal matters. A Process Server must serve said documents in accordance with court directives in order for the serve to be considered legal and valid. With professionalism and excellent communication, we will ensure the documents relevant to your case will be accurately and punctually delivered to the correct recipient(s).

Corporate or Privately contracted, our team is committed to providing outstanding surveillance services without compromising accuracy, reliability, or the law. With secure surveillance evidence obtained during our investigations, we strive to provide accurate and relevant documentation to support the legal proceedings of a case.

Investigating a partner is never a simple decision. Because we understand this, we take discretion and confidentiality to the highest level.
